Common questions

Do we need Copilot licenses before the assessment?
No. The assessment is designed to tell you whether you're ready for Copilot. We'll identify what needs fixing first, so you're not paying for licenses while you sort out data governance issues.
How long does the readiness assessment take?
Typically a few days to a couple of weeks, depending on the size of your organisation and complexity of your M365 environment. We'll give you a clear timeline after an initial conversation.
Is our data safe? Will it be used to train AI models?
Microsoft Copilot only accesses data within your secure environment. Your data is not used to train public AI models. Part of our assessment ensures your internal data security is configured correctly.
What if we've already rolled out Copilot but adoption is low?
That's common. We can run a training and adoption programme to get your team actually using what you've paid for. It usually starts with understanding why adoption stalled and addressing those specific barriers.
Can you help with Power Platform automation too?
Yes. Our Custom Agents & Automation service covers Copilot Studio, Power Automate, and AI Builder. If you're looking to automate workflows or build custom AI assistants, we can help scope and build those solutions.
